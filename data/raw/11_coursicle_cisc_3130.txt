Description
4 hours; 4 creditsContainer classes: their design, implementations, and applications. Sequences: vectors, linked lists, stacks, queues, deques, lists. Associative structures: sets, maps and their hash and tree underlying representations. Sorting and searching techniques. Collection frameworks and hierarchies. Prerequisite: Computer and Information Science 3115; or 3110 and either 1115 or 1170. Enrollment Requirements: COMPUTER AND INFORMATION SCIENCE 3115; OR 3110 AND EITHER 1115 OR 1170, WITH A GRADE OF C OR HIGHER, IS REQUIRED TO TAKE THIS COURSE. Requirement Designation: Regular Liberal Arts.
